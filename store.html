The Girl of Ink & Stars

.by Kiran Millwood Hargrave

Tags :

Adventure Fiction Mystery Friendship Young Adult Fantasy Enchanting World Exploration

Description :

"The Girl of Ink & Stars" by Kiran Millwood Hargrave is a captivating young adult fantasy novel set on the mysterious island of Joya. The story follows Isabella, a courageous young girl determined to uncover the truth behind the disappearance of her best friend. As she ventures into forbidden territories, Isabella discovers ancient secrets, encounters mythical creatures, and confronts the oppressive forces that have kept her village in fear. Filled with themes of friendship, courage, and the power of storytelling, this beautifully crafted tale transports readers to a richly imagined world brimming with adventure and magic, making it a compelling read for audiences of all ages.
